Tom Yum Goong: A Spicy Thai Delight

Ingredients:

Lemongrass: 2 stalks","Thai basil: 1 cup","kaffir Lime Leaves: 3 leaves", and "shrimp: 500g".

1/4 cup of fish sauce adds authentic Thai flavor. 1 tablespoon of chili paste gives the soup its signature heat.
